Cloud-Based Korean Kitchen

Project Description

Chicken Today is a delivery-only Korean fried chicken restaurant powered by cloud-based SaaS tools. The goal is to manage orders, delivery, and branding efficiently through online platforms while offering customers high-quality, fast Korean food in Los Angeles.

Target Audience / Market

Our primary audience includes people around Koreatown, Los Angeles, who crave authentic Korean fried chicken delivered conveniently to their doorsteps. We aim to solve the problem of long wait times and limited authentic Korean food options for delivery.
